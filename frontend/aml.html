1. 개요

㈜굳프렌즈(이하 "회사")는 자금세탁 및 테러자금 조달을 방지하기 위해 관련 법령을 준수하며, 국제 기준에 부합하는 자금세탁방지(AML) 정책을 시행합니다.

Trading-X는 주문 편의 도구 플랫폼이며, 이용자의 자금을 직접 보유·수탁·관리하지 않습니다. 모든 자금의 입출금은 이용자가 선택한 MT5 브로커를 통해 이루어집니다.

  • 대한민국 「공중 등 협박목적 및 대량살상무기확산을 위한 자금조달행위의 금지에 관한 법률」
  • 국제자금세탁방지기구(FATF) 권고사항

회사는 관련 법령의 개정 시 본 정책을 지체 없이 업데이트합니다.

3. 서비스 구조 및 기본 원칙

Trading-X의 서비스 구조상 다음의 기본 원칙이 적용됩니다.

  • Trading-X는 주문 편의 도구이며, 이용자의 자금을 직접 보유·수탁·관리하지 않습니다

4. 고객확인(KYC) 절차

Trading-X의 서비스 구조상 KYC(Know Your Customer) 절차는 다음과 같이 수행됩니다.

  • 본인 인증 및 신원 확인은 이용자가 연동하는 MT5 브로커에서 수행합니다
  • Trading-X는 브로커의 KYC 결과에 의존하며, 별도의 신원 확인 절차를 수행하지 않습니다
  • 회사는 필요 시 추가적인 본인 확인을 요청할 수 있습니다

이용자는 브로커의 KYC 절차를 성실히 이행해야 하며, Trading-X 계정과 MT5 브로커 계정의 명의가 동일해야 합니다.

5. 이용자 의무

서비스를 이용하는 모든 이용자는 다음의 의무를 준수해야 합니다.

  • 본인 명의로만 계정을 생성하고 서비스를 이용해야 합니다

  • 타인의 계정을 대리 사용하거나 양도·대여해서는 안 됩니다
  • 불법적으로 취득한 자금을 이용한 거래를 해서는 안 됩니다
  • 자금세탁 또는 테러자금 조달에 해당하는 활동을 인지한 경우 회사에 신고해야 합니다

1. Overview

GOODFRIENDS Co., Ltd. (hereinafter "the Company") complies with relevant laws and regulations to prevent money laundering and terrorist financing, and implements an Anti-Money Laundering (AML) policy that meets international standards.

Trading-X is a utility platform for order convenience and does not hold, custody, or manage users' funds. All fund deposits and withdrawals are processed through the MT5 broker selected by the user.

Effective Date: January 5, 2026   |   Last Updated: January 5, 2026

2. Applicable Laws and Standards

This policy is based on the following laws and international standards:

  • Act on Reporting and Using Specified Financial Transaction Information (Republic of Korea)
  • Act on the Prohibition of Financing for Offences of Public Intimidation and Proliferation of Weapons of Mass Destruction (Republic of Korea)
  • Financial Action Task Force (FATF) Recommendations

The Company updates this policy promptly when relevant laws and regulations are amended.

3. Service Structure and Basic Principles

The following basic principles apply based on Trading-X's service structure:

  • Trading-X is an order convenience tool and does not hold, custody, or manage users' funds
  • All fund deposits and withdrawals are processed through the MT5 broker selected by the user
  • The Company prohibits the use of the service for illegal purposes
  • Trading-X connects to brokers through MetaApi OÜ (Estonia) and is not involved in direct fund transfer channels

4. Know Your Customer (KYC) Procedures

KYC procedures within Trading-X's service structure are performed as follows:

  • Identity verification is performed by the MT5 broker connected by the user
  • Trading-X relies on the broker's KYC results and does not perform separate identity verification procedures
  • The Company may request additional identity verification when necessary

Users must faithfully complete the broker's KYC procedures, and the Trading-X account and MT5 broker account must be under the same identity.

5. User Obligations

All users of the service must comply with the following obligations:

  • Create accounts and use the service only under their own identity
  • Provide accurate and up-to-date information during registration
  • Must not use, transfer, or lend another person's account
  • Must not engage in transactions using illegally obtained funds
  • Must report to the Company any activities suspected of money laundering or terrorist financing

6. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited and may result in account suspension and legal action.

  • Using the service for money laundering, terrorist financing, or other illegal fund transfers
  • Creating accounts using another person's identity or false information
  • Transactions intended to conceal or disguise proceeds of crime
  • Using the service for transactions involving countries, organizations, or individuals subject to UN, EU, OFAC, or other international sanctions
  • Using the service to transfer or receive illegal funds to or from third parties

7. Transaction Monitoring

The Company may conduct the following monitoring to prevent money laundering and illegal activities:

  • Detection of abnormal trading patterns or usage behavior
  • Identification of repetitive or unusually large transactions within short periods
  • Internal review of suspicious account activities

Monitoring is conducted based on users' trading activity data within the service and does not involve direct access to users' broker account funds.

8. Company Actions

The Company may restrict or suspend account access without prior notice in the following cases:

  • When suspicious activities are detected
  • When a user violates this policy or applicable laws
  • When requested by investigative authorities or the Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU)
  • When a user is identified on international sanctions lists

The Company fully cooperates with investigative and regulatory authorities in accordance with applicable laws.

9. Record Retention

The Company retains the following records in accordance with applicable laws:

  • Account creation and identity verification records: 5 years after account closure
  • Transaction records: 5 years after transaction completion
  • Suspicious transaction report records: 5 years after reporting

10. Policy Changes

The Company may amend this policy due to changes in applicable laws or service operational needs. When the policy is amended, the changes and effective date will be notified in advance through in-service announcements or email.
